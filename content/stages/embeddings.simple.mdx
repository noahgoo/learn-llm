A token ID is just a dictionary position — the number 3797 says nothing
about what " cat" *means*. The embedding step gives each token meaning: the
model looks up row 3797 in a giant learned table and gets back a list of
768 numbers — a point in a 768-dimensional space.<Cite id="radford2019" />

During training, tokens used in similar ways drifted close together in
that space. " cat" sits near " dog" and " kitten"; " Paris" sits near
" London". Meaning becomes geometry: directions and distances you can
measure.

The cloud around you is that space (flattened to 3-D so we can fly through
it). Your tokens light up at their positions — see what the model considers
their neighbors.
