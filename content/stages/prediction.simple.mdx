At the end, the model does not directly choose a word. It scores every
token in its vocabulary. Those raw scores are called logits.

Softmax turns the logits into probabilities, so the highest scores become
the most likely next tokens.<Cite id="vaswani2017" />

Temperature changes how bold the model is. Low temperature concentrates
probability on the top choices. High temperature spreads probability out,
which can make text more varied but also less reliable.<Cite id="holtzman2020" />

Generation is this same move repeated. The model samples one token, appends
it to the text, then predicts again from the longer sequence until it hits
a stop token or length limit.<Cite id="radford2019" />
