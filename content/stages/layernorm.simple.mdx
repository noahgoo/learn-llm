LayerNorm keeps a token's signal in a workable range. If some channels get
too large while others shrink, the next layer can become harder to use.
LayerNorm recenters and rescales the token vector.<Cite id="ba2016" />

The bars show a compact before-and-after view. The exact details are
technical, but the intuition is simple: steady signals are easier for the
next part of the model to read.
