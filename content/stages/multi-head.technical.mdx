GPT-2 small uses 12 attention heads per layer. Each head has dimension
d<sub>k</sub> = 64, so the 768-wide residual stream is split into parallel
Q/K/V projections, attended independently, concatenated, and projected
back through W<sub>O</sub>.<Cite id="vaswani2017" />

The head gallery visualizes the live softmax matrices for one layer. These
patterns are real model outputs for your prompt, but head interpretation
should be treated carefully: analysis and pruning work shows some heads
are more important or specialized than others, while many do not map to a
single clean human label.<Cite id="michel2019" />

After concatenation, the output projection mixes head results into one
768-dimensional write to the residual stream.
