GPT-2 small uses 12 attention heads per layer. The 768-wide residual
stream is not cut into fixed chunks; learned matrices first project it
into Q/K/V vectors, and those projections are reshaped into parallel head
spaces with d<sub>k</sub> = 64. Each head attends independently, then the
head outputs are concatenated and projected back through W<sub>O</sub>.<Cite id="vaswani2017" />

The head gallery visualizes the live softmax matrices for one layer. These
patterns are real model outputs for your prompt, but head interpretation
should be treated carefully: analysis and pruning work shows some heads
are more important or specialized than others, while many do not map to a
single clean human label.<Cite id="michel2019" />

After concatenation, the output projection mixes head results into one
768-dimensional write to the residual stream.

Many modern decoder LLMs keep many query heads but share fewer key/value
heads through grouped-query attention. That keeps several query views
while shrinking the KV cache used during long-context generation.<Cite id="ainslie2023" />
