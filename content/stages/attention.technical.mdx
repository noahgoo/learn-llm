Each head projects the (LayerNormed) residual stream with learned matrices:
**Q = XW<sub>Q</sub>**, **K = XW<sub>K</sub>**, **V = XW<sub>V</sub>**,
with head dimension d<sub>k</sub> = 768/12 = 64. The output is

**Attention(Q, K, V) = softmax(QKᵀ / √d<sub>k</sub> + M) V**

— raw affinities QKᵀ are scaled by √d<sub>k</sub> (without it, dot
products grow with dimension and push softmax into vanishing-gradient
saturation<Cite id="vaswani2017" />), then the causal mask **M** sets
every position j &gt; i to −∞ so softmax assigns it exactly zero: GPT-2
is a decoder-only model and may not condition on the future.<Cite id="radford2019" />

Each softmax row is a probability distribution — the live matrix you're
viewing; rows sum to 1. It multiplies V to give each position a convex
combination of value vectors, which is then projected back and **added**
to the residual stream — attention *writes* its findings rather than
replacing anything.<Cite id="elhage2021" />

Switch heads in the selector: the same Q/K/V mechanism, twelve different
learned projections — the subject of the next stage.
